# Welcome to Hibuno

Hi there! We're Hibuno, your new favorite destination for free, powerful web tools that make digital tasks simpler and more efficient.

![Hibuno Tools Collection](/images/hibuno-tools-banner.jpg)

## Our Mission

At Hibuno, we believe that powerful digital tools should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to create a comprehensive suite of web-based utilities that help you work smarter, not harder—all without requiring downloads, installations, or subscriptions.

> We build tools that respect your privacy, value your time, and enhance your productivity.

Every tool we create is designed with three core principles in mind: simplicity, efficiency, and accessibility. Whether you're a professional developer, a content creator, a student, or just someone who occasionally needs to manipulate text or images, our tools are built for you.

## What We Offer

### Text Tools

Our comprehensive text manipulation suite includes over 20 specialized tools for transforming, cleaning, and analyzing text:

- **Case Transformations**: Convert text to lowercase, UPPERCASE, Title Case, and more
- **Line Operations**: Sort, reverse, shuffle, number, and deduplicate lines
- **Content Extraction**: Pull emails, URLs, and numbers from any text
- **Text Cleaning**: Remove whitespace, punctuation, HTML tags, and more

### Image Tools

Transform and optimize your images with our powerful browser-based utilities:

- **Image Compression**: Reduce file sizes while maintaining quality
- **Format Conversion**: Convert between WebP, AVIF, JPEG, PNG, and more
- **Background Removal**: Automatically remove image backgrounds using AI

### Coming Soon

We're constantly expanding our toolkit. Here's what's coming next:

- **Color Tools**: Color pickers, palette generators, and accessibility checkers
- **Code Utilities**: Formatters, minifiers, and syntax highlighters
- **PDF Tools**: Merge, split, compress, and convert PDF files

## Privacy First

We take your privacy seriously. All our tools run directly in your browser—your files and data never leave your device. There's no tracking, no data collection, and no compromises.
