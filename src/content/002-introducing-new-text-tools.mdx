# Introducing New Text Tools

We're excited to announce several new additions to our text manipulation toolkit, designed to make your content creation and editing workflows even more efficient.

![Text Tools Collection](/images/text-tools-banner.jpg)

## New Text Transformation Features

### Advanced Case Conversion

Our case conversion tools have been expanded to include:

- **Sentence case**: Automatically capitalizes the first letter of each sentence while converting the rest to lowercase
- **Alternating case**: Transforms text into aLtErNaTiNg CaSe for creative styling
- **Random case**: Creates randomly capitalized text for unique visual effects

### Smart Text Extraction

Extract specific types of content from your text with our new intelligent extraction tools:

- **Date Extraction**: Automatically identifies and extracts dates in various formats
- **Phone Number Extraction**: Pulls phone numbers from text, recognizing different international formats
- **Custom Pattern Extraction**: Define your own regex patterns to extract exactly what you need

### Markdown Tools

For content creators and developers working with Markdown:

- **HTML to Markdown**: Convert HTML content to clean, well-formatted Markdown
- **Markdown to HTML**: Transform your Markdown into properly structured HTML
- **Markdown Formatter**: Clean up and standardize your Markdown documents

## Enhanced Text Analysis

We've also added new analysis tools to help you understand your text better:

- **Readability Scores**: Get Flesch-Kincaid, SMOG, and other readability metrics
- **Keyword Density**: Identify the most frequently used words and phrases
- **Character Distribution**: Visualize the distribution of characters in your text

## Privacy-First Approach

As with all our tools, these new text features process everything locally in your browser. Your text never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security for your content.

## How to Access

All these new tools are available now in our [Text Tools](/tools/text-tools) section. They're completely free to use, with no account required.

We're constantly working to improve our tools based on user feedback. If you have suggestions for additional text manipulation features you'd like to see, please let us know!
